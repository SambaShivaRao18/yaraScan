Steps to Use the YARA-based Malware Scanner

  1. Visit the Home Page: Navigate to the home page of the YARASCAN website.
  2. Upload a File: Click on the "Upload" button to select a file from your computer that you want to scan for malware.
  3. Scan the File: After uploading the file, click on the "Scan" button to start the scanning process. The system will use YARA rules to analyze the file.
  4. View Scan Results: Once the scan is complete, you will be redirected to the "Scan Results" page where you can see the results of the scan. If any malware signatures are matched, they will be displayed with details.
  5. Review Matched Rules: In the scan results, you can see the specific YARA rules that matched any potential malware signatures in your file.
  6. Stay Informed: Regularly check for updates on the YARASCAN website to stay informed about new features and improvements.

For any questions or support, please contact our support team through the contact information provided on our website.
